# Why off-the-shelf AI stalls in regulated work

Generic AI tools are remarkable in a sandbox and disappointing in a clinic. The
reason isn't the model — it's everything around it.

## The demo is the easy 20%

A chatbot that sounds competent is a solved problem. The hard 80% is the part no
demo shows: connecting to the calendar, respecting the records system, routing
to the right clinician, honouring the rules a regulated practice can't bend.

## The integration gap

Off-the-shelf products stop at the edge of their own box. Real clinics run on a
stack that was never designed to be plugged into — and the value only appears
once the AI is wired *into* that stack, not bolted beside it.
